## High-value extraction targets
- Story map as whole-picture preservation mechanism
- Story map construction steps and backbone/activity/task decomposition
- MVP slicing and release slicing rules
- Validation-learning loops linked to story mapping
- Story-writing anti-patterns such as template zombie behavior
- Boundaries for what should and should not be written on story cards
- Story workshop and collaborative refinement patterns

## Skip or low-value sections
- Publishing metadata, copyright, CIP, acknowledgements
- Long motivational passages in prefaces when they do not add reusable method structure
- Repetitive praise of story mapping as a concept without new operational guidance

## Extraction strategy notes
- This book should be treated primarily as a `method-heavy` source, not a template-heavy source.
- Priority cards should be:
  - method cards
  - anti-pattern cards
  - checklist cards
  - boundary cards
- Avoid over-extracting narrative examples unless they clarify a reusable technique.
