## Anti-pattern cards

- counting tests instead of judging protection quality
- assigning exact scores with no evidence
- letting the scorecard make the decision without a risk-to-action rationale
- listing debt without ranking or next-step consequence
- saying “safe to change” while hotspot modules remain unprotected
- calling a mixed behavior change “technical refactor” with no explicit boundary
